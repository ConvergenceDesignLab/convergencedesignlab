Convergence & Columbia College

We are an innovation incubator in the School of Media Arts (SMA) at Columbia College Chicago. Columbia aims to be an animating force in our Chicago communities and in the larger world beyond. Convergence works to make this promise a reality by fostering deep connections between the college and the cultural, media, and learning sectors locally, nationally and globally.

Convergence provides opportunities for the college to lead in area-wide conversations about emerging digital economies and information literacies while continuing to foster a strategic vision for Chicagos connected city. Together, Convergence and SMA are exploring how digital technologies are transforming society and culture, producing new forms of interaction, and modeling new information ecosystems, and using that knowledge to design the future of participation, and empower and transform communities across the greater Chicago area.
